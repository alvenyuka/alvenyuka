## 👋 About Me

I'm a **Finance × Data Science practitioner** building production-grade ML systems and financial models that bridge quantitative analysis with real-world business impact. Based in **Nairobi, Kenya**, I specialise in the FinTech vertical — fraud detection, credit risk, customer analytics — using Python, machine learning, and advanced Excel financial modelling.

- 🎯 Fraud detection system achieving **0.674 PR-AUC** on highly imbalanced data (1.8% fraud rate)
- 🔍 SHAP explainability for **regulatory compliance** (KYC/AML reporting requirements)
- 📊 CFA-level risk analytics in Excel: **CAPM · VaR · CVaR · Sharpe · Sortino · Calmar**
- 🏡 Australian tax-optimised mortgage strategy modelling **$2M+ lifetime tax savings**
